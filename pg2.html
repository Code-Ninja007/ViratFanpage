Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. Known for his fierce competitiveness and passion for the game, Virat has shattered numerous records across all formats of cricket.

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Virat started his cricketing journey at a young age. He quickly rose through the ranks, making his international debut in 2008. Over the years, his consistency and extraordinary performances have earned him a place among the legends of the sport.

As a batsman, Kohli is known for his excellent technique, mental toughness, and the ability to chase down targets. He has scored centuries in some of the most challenging conditions, proving his mettle time and again. Off the field, Virat is also known for his philanthropic efforts and commitment to fitness, inspiring millions around the world.
